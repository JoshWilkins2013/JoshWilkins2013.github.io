Synthesizer

Designed a high frequency synthesizer by implementing a cascaded PLL with an output power level controller. It was optimized to minimize phase noise and the results were over 10 times better than commercially available components. The enclosure was created using interchangeable RF shielded rack mount units to minimize EMI effects. There are two reasons for it's unreasonable size: The first reason, is that the design consists entirely of connectorized eval PCBs. The second reason, is that my (much smaller) design of the enclosure was turned down to ensure post-construction configurability.
