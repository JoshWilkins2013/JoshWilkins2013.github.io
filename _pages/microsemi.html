After I finished my undergraduate degree, I left Carestream and went to work for Microsemi in Beverly, MA. This branch of Microsemi is the Frequency and Timing Division (FTD). Their primary focus is on the creation of the most accurate and precise atomic clocks (in the world). Such precision creates many challenges in the design process and requires the utmost attention to detail. Not long after I started working for Microsemi, they were acquired by Microchip (early 2018).
