VArm

A partial body immersion device for VR which allows the user to physically interact with virtual objects. By manipulating the user's arm in response to virtual interactions with objects, a form of kinesthetic feedback is provided. The initial design was constructed with what are known as air muscles.

Ambilight

Utilizing a Raspberry Pi (RPI), a dynamic lighting effect corresponding to the TV's video content was created. In this build, I used Rasplex to stream video from the RPI and Hyperion to implement the lighting backrop. It is capable of producing the color spectrum at different shades, brightness, and intensities based upon frame content.
